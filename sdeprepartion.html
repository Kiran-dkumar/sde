üéØ Success Tips for Working Professionals

  • Consistency over Intensity: 3.5 hours daily is better than sporadic long sessions
  • Active Learning: Write code by hand during morning sessions to improve retention
  • Weekend Deep Dives: Use weekends for comprehensive practice and review
  • Mock Interviews: Practice with friends or online platforms weekly from Month 2
  • Track Progress: Maintain a problem-solving log and review weekly
  • Health First: Don't compromise sleep - early morning sessions require 7+ hours sleep
  • Spaced Repetition: Revisit problems after 1 week, 1 month intervals
  • Company Research: Start researching target companies from Month 3
  • Network Building: Connect with professionals in target companies
  • Backup Plans: Apply to multiple companies, don't put all eggs in one basket
  • Rejection Recovery: Learn from feedback, stay motivated, every "no" brings you closer to "yes"
  • Communication is Key: Technical skills get you to the interview, communication skills get you the job
